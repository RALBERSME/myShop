It is your choice to stop your thoughts and to ask yourself if yourself thoughts are really true. If they are not true - we can let them go. If they are still true for us - we are now aware of them and we can handle them the way WE want. We are not our feelings. WE decide about our feelings.
It is up to us to choose every moment if we want to be happy or not.
